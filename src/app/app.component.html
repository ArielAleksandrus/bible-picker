What's the kind of selection you want? Use in select

We allow 'book', 'books', 'chapter', 'chapters', 'verse', 'verses' and 'any' values. 'any' means that the user has to double click the book to open the chapters page, and double click the chapter to open the verses page, and at any time the user can click 'Ok' to finish selecting.
